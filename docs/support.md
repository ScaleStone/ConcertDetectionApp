# ConcertSongFinder Support

ConcertSongFinder organizes your concert videos and photos by the songs
playing in them.

## Getting started

1. Open the app and go to the **Upload** tab.
2. Select the videos and photos from a concert (or several concerts —
   they'll be separated automatically).
3. Analysis runs automatically: songs are identified, the concert and its
   setlist are looked up, and everything lands in **My Concerts**.
4. Open a concert to browse its media by song, search by song name, and
   share clips tagged with the song.

## Common questions

**A concert wasn't identified.**
The app labels it by artist and date instead. You can re-run analysis by
re-importing the media — identification improves when the setlist service
is reachable.

**A song is labeled "Unknown."**
Loud crowds and distorted audio can defeat recognition. Unknown media is
still kept with its concert and searchable under "Unknown."

**Sharing to Instagram.**
Use the share button on any video or photo and keep "Include song caption"
on — the song name is drawn onto the media so it survives social uploads.
